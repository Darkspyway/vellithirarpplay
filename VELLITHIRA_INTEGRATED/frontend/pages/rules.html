City Rules

General Rules

1
No RDM/VDM

Random Deathmatch and Vehicle Deathmatch are strictly prohibited. Always have a valid reason.

2
FailRP

Always stay in character. If you need to speak out of character, use /b or /me for context.

3
Metagaming

Transferring OOC information to IC situations is a bannable offense.

4
Powergaming

Do not force actions on others or perform impossible tasks (e.g., flipping a car by hand).

5
New Life Rule (NLR)

If you die, you forget all events leading up to your death and cannot return for 15 minutes.

6
Exploiting

Using game bugs or third-party software for an advantage results in a permanent ban.

7
Mic Requirement

A high-quality working microphone is mandatory for all players in the city.

8
Combat Logging

Disconnecting during an active roleplay scenario or chase is strictly forbidden.

9
OOC Toxicity

Keep out-of-character arguments away from public channels. Respect all community members.

10
Staff Authority

Decisions made by staff during reports are final. Appeal through the proper Discord channels.

Gang Rules

1
Membership Caps

Gangs are limited to 15 active members. Roster changes must be reported to staff.

2
Turf Protocol

Conflicts over territory must have clear roleplay build-up and mutual agreement.

3
Identifying Uniforms

Gangs must wear a consistent color or identifiable piece of clothing during active crimes.

4
Alliance Limits

No more than two gangs can ally for a single operation or conflict.

5
Hostility Build-up

War cannot be declared instantly. There must be at least 24 hours of IC tension.

6
Drive-by Rules

Drive-bys must be brief and targeted. Spraying crowded areas is considered FailRP.

7
Ransom Limits

Kidnapping ransoms must be realistic ($10,000 max for civilians, $50,000 for high-ranking officials).

8
Leaving a Gang

Exiting a gang requires a "Blood Out" scenario, which may involve a character memory wipe.

9
Gang-on-Gang Respect

Rude behavior without reason isn't RP. Conflict needs substance, not just "because we're rivals."

10
Initiation

Criminal acts against others must start with clear verbal initiation.

Citizen Rules

1
Value Your Life (NVL)

Always act as if your life is in danger when faced with superior force or weaponry.

2
Realistic Driving

Perform stunts or off-roading only if your vehicle is realistically capable of doing so.

3
Interaction with Emergency Services

Respect PD and EMS. Interfering with their work without a strong reason is forbidden.

4
Public Disturbance

Avoid causing mass chaos in high-traffic social zones unless it's an approved event.

5
Scamming Limits

Scamming players for items over $20,000 is not permitted. Only IC trades are protected.

6
Property Respect

Do not enter private residences or businesses without permission or a criminal intent RP.

7
Job Professionalism

While on duty as a legal worker, maintain a level of professionalism expected for that role.

8
Non-Combat Zones

Main hospitals and police stations are safe zones. No violence allowed within their perimeters.

9
Reporting Crimes

If you witness a crime, report it realistically. Don't chase criminals yourself as a civilian.

10
Civilian Gear

Citizens cannot dress as or use equipment exclusive to PD/EMS (e.g., body armor or sirens).

Streamer Rules

1
Zero Tolerance for Stream Sniping

Using a live feed to find or target a player will result in a permanent ban.

2
Toxicity Threshold

Maintain a positive environment. OOC rants about other players on stream are prohibited.

3
DMCA Awareness

Be cautious with copyrighted music. The server is not responsible for stream strikes.

4
Chat Meta Management

Ignored chat info. Using chat-provided data in-game is considered Metagaming.

5
Staff Confidentiality

Do not discuss active staff tickets or bans on your live broadcast.

6
Sensitive Content

Warn your audience and follow platform guidelines during intense or dark RP scenes.

7
Player Privacy

Avoid leaking Discord IDs or personal info of fellow players while streaming.

8
Interaction Quality

Ensure your streaming doesn't hinder your ability to provide quality RP to others.

9
VOD Recording

Keeping VODs or recordings is encouraged as evidence for potential rule disputes.

10
Streamer Identity

Being a streamer does not grant special privileges. Everyone follows the same rules.

Robbery Rules

1
Negotiation Preference

Give police a chance to negotiate before resorting to gunfire.

2
Hostage Value

Treat hostages with care. Their safety is your primary leverage in negotiations.

3
Max Participants

Robberies are limited to 4 participants for small stores and 6 for major banks.

4
Cooldown Timer

Players must wait 60 minutes between starting different robbery scenarios.

5
Getaway Vehicles

Avoid using unrealistic "super-cars" or heavily armored vehicles for every heist.

6
Weapon Restrictions

Small robberies (Convenience stores) are limited to pistols and melee only.

7
Police Interaction

Do not "bait" cops into a chase just for the fun of it. Have a criminal goal.

8
Surrender Mechanics

If you are cornered and outmatched, surrendering is often the best roleplay choice.

9
Looting Limits

Only take what is realistically portable. Don't empty an entire bank's inventory in seconds.

10
Hostage Consent

Do not force players into hostage situations for hours. Keep the RP moving.

Club Rules

1
Business Registration

All clubs must be registered with the city government to operate legally.

2
Social Priority

Clubs are designed for social interaction. Keep heavy crime and combat outside.

3
Staff Management

Owners are responsible for the actions and payroll of their security and bartenders.

4
Safety Protocols

Clubs must employ certified security to ensure the safety of all patrons.

5
Alcohol Roleplay

Patrons should roleplay the effects of excessive drinking realistically.

6
Event Coordination

Major parties or live music events should be scheduled with staff for server stability.

7
Noise Ordinance

While clubs are loud, respect neighboring businesses in character.

8
Dress Codes

Owners can enforce IC dress codes for their establishments.

9
Competitive Spirit

Business rivalry is encouraged through marketing, not through arson or violence.

10
Underage RP

Strictly follow server age protocols for entry into nightlife establishments.
